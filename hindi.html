HINDI






A. Tips to write Hindi Paper


1. Presentation is Important

Maintain an appropriate pace and handwriting throughout the paper. A good Presentation will certainly cast a good impression on the Moderator. To improve and Maintain this see the Presentation of the Boards Topper paper. This will be very helpful to score good marks.


2. Give Real life Examples in Swamats.

Swamat writing should br done in such a way that we compare daily life events in it. Give ongoing real life examples in it. This trick will help you to score out off marks in Swamats.


3. Use Writer's and Poet's name in Swamat and Saral arth

Using Writer's and Poet's name in Swamat and Saral arth will make it different from others. This will show Moderator that you have read the lesson and have good knowledge of Writers and lessons. In order to score more you need to learn all Writers and Poets name for all lessons.


4. Use Quality words

Using high vocabulary will help you to improve Quality of the Writing skill topics. For this learn all complex words from the textbook and use them while practicing sample papers..


5. Avoid Spelling Mistakes

If your Swamat or any answer is very good but it contains spelling mistakes then it can spoil your whole answer. If you don't know spelling of any of the word then use similar word of it but don't do spelling mistakes. Thus practice it more and try to avoid it.


6. Try to use Perfect Punctuation wherever Necessary

Give Attention to your punctuation marks as many Hindi words have puctuation in it. While reading textbook read the words with proper puctuation; you can find that every 10 out of 2 words contains punctuations like '.', '-' etc. Mastering this will help you to score very good marks in it.


7. Maintain Quality and Flow of Language

Keep all the answers to the point and maintain flow of language. Also focus on the Quality of the answers.


8. Don't Attempt the unknown topics first

If you don't know any of the question of a section ex:-poetry then attempt it at last (jo nahi atta last me attempt karo) . If you attempt unknown topics first then it will create bad impression on Moderator and he will deduct your marks further.



B. Preperation Tips


1. Write 10 lines on Evergreen Topics everyday

Practicing 10 lines on each Evergreen Topic will help you to improve thinking ability. This trick will help you to score good marks in Writing skills.(You can Evergreen Topics on youtube)


2. Practice Swamats.

Practice Swamats as it contains 10 marks in the whole paper. So read all the topics under each exercise (don't byhart them). Read them and understand the topic. Reading many topics can help in Swamats and Writing skills.


3. Focus on Poem's meaning line-by-line

See Animated lectures of Poems as many time as you can and do revision of it systematically. For poems, you need to search it's deeper meaning. Poems are hard but have many hidden meanings inside it.


4. Learn all Grammar and Vocabulary Topics

Focus on grammar and vocabulary as it contains 22 marks. Grammar contains 14 and vcabulary 8 marks. Practice grammar from sample paper and vocabulary from digest while practicing passages. Do this and secure your 22 Marks.


5. Focus on Writing Speed

Hindi paper is lenghty as compared to Marathi and English practice at least 5-6 sample papers of Hindi to improve writing speed. To improve speed and solve paper on time you need to practice sample papers.
