A. Tips to write Marathi Paper


1. Presentation is Important

Maintain an appropriate pace and handwriting throughout the paper. A good Presentation will certainly cast a good impression on the Moderator. To improve and maintain this see the Presentation of the Boards Topper paper. This will be very helpful to score good Marks.


2. First Impression

As I have said in English, Marathi paper's First Impression is also very important. Really Moderator will judge you by looking at your first page. Thus keep it neat and clean.


3. Use Writer's and Poet's name in Swamat and Bhavarth

Using Writer's and Poet's name in Swamat and Bhavarth will make it different from others. This will show Moderator that you have read the lesson and have good knowledge of writers and lessons. In order to score more you need to learn all Writers and Poets name for all lessons.


4. Write Prastavna in Swamat and Abhivyakti

Below all the chapters there is a box which contains Prastavna which contains Summary and Theme of the lesson in few lines. B. Preperation Tips


1. Learn Vakprachar from Digest of all lessons

Learn all Vakprachar from the Digest or Masterkey that are given below each lessons. Byhart all of them and secure your 4 Marks.


2. Reading many examples of Writing skills

Reading and practicing more and more topics of letters, stories, reports will help you improve vocabulary and good knowledge of topics which will ultimately help in English exam.


3. Focus on Grammar

Focus more on the grammar topics because Marathi grammar is much more easier than Hindi and English. So you can easily score out off marks in this section.


4. Extra Tip for non Non-Marathi friends

Always talk to your Marathi friends in Marathi. This will help you to improve your Marathi speaking skills and ultimately you will be good writer of Marathi. Don't even think that only Marathi students can get good marks in Marathi Subject.
