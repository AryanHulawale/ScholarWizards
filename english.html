ENGLISH






A. Tips to write English Paper


1. Presentation is important

Maintain an appropriate pace and handwriting throughout the paper. A good Presentation will certainly cast a good impression on the Moderator. You may underline the bullet points you wish to make – this saves a lot of Moderator's time. Attempt all the questions in a proper sequence- section a, section b and section c. This will help alot.


2. First Impression

First Impression is very important as your first page puts good or bad impact on Moderator. Moderator will judge you by looking at your first page; Thus keep first page neat and clean. First Impression will help you the most in language paper to score good marks.


3. Answer to the point

Write answer to the point, don't over exeed any of the topic in the answers. Don't think that Quantity of the answer will help to score good marks. If you over exeed any topic then Moderator will deduct your marks.


4. Quantity < Quality

Quality of the answers is more important than answer's length. Here is example of This ex:- a. Water is very important resource. We should save it. We should use it properly | b. Water is our Biological need. There is only 3% fresh water; out of which 2% is covered with glacier. Here 'b' will get more Marks.


5. Highlight Keywords

You may underline the bullet points you wish to make – this saves a lot of Moderator's time. Highlight the important points like main event in poem appreciation, story, report and other Writing Skills topics.


6. Don't Attempt the unknown topics first

If you don't know any of the question of a section ex:-poetry then attempt it at last ( jo nahi atta last me attempt karo ). If you attempt unknown topics first then it will create bad impression on Moderator and he will deduct your marks further. I got huge help due to this in Hindi subject.


7. Avoid Spelling Mistakes

If your answer is very good but it contains spelling mistakes then it can spoil your whole answer. If you don't know spelling of any of the word then use similar word of it but avoid spelling mistakes. Thus practice it and try to avoid it.


8. Flow of Language

Maintain the flow of the language; don't change the topic immediately. Slowly change the topic to the topic which is related to that. This trick will be Helpful in Writing Skills.


9. Use Rough Paper to Write Ideas

Before writing any topic of Writing Skill write down ideas on rough paper and then start writing Writing Skill. This will help to improve writing Accuracy.



B. Preperation Tips


1. Reading many examples of Writing Skills

Reading and practicing more and more topics of letters, stories, reports will help you improve vocabulary and good knowledge of topics which will ultimately help in English exam.


2. Learn High Vocabulary

Using high vocabulary will help you to improve Quality of the Writing Skill topics. For this learn all complex words from the textbook and use them while practicing sample papers.


3. Sample Paper Practice

Using sample papers, students will witness a boost in confidence with regular practice, answering questions that they can answer and realising how well-prepared they are for the test. The exam sample paper can make students prepare for board exams or any regular test convenient to appear.
